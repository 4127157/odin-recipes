Lasagna

Description

When referring to them in plural they are funnily called 'lasagne'.

Its a type of pasta and thought to be the oldest. Made with wide, flat sheets of pasta.

Ingredients

  • Italian Seasoning
  • Garlic
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Create Layers
  2. Sauce each layer
  3. Add choice of veggies with garlic
  4. Add cheese in as many layers sparingly
  5. On the top layer there should be loads
  6. Bake it like a pizza and you're done!
