Banana Bread

Description

An absolute classic!

For when you don't want to feel like you're stuffing up on cake by thinking its just bread!

Ingredients

  • Half cup butter melted
  • One cup white sugar
  • Two eggs
  • One teaspoon vanilla extract
  • One and half cup all-purpose flour
  • One teaspoon baking soda
  • Half teaspoon salt
  • Half cup sour cream
  • Half cup chopped walnuts
  • Two medium bananans sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 175 C and grease pan
  2. Stir everything in a large bowl
  3. Bake for 60 mins and you're done lol
