Savoury Crepes

Description

Crepes are a great way to start the day.

They are one of the most versatile when it comes to combinations, much like tacos.

Whether you want a hearty or light, it doesn't matter.

Ingredients

  • One and a half cup milk
  • One cup all-purpose flour
  • Two large eggs
  • Two tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • One tablespoon melted butter
  • Two teaspoons chilly garlic sauce(Sriracha works)

Steps

  1. Combine Ingredients in a blender
  2. Heat crepe pan
  3. Flip crepe to the brown side
  4. Whisk eggs until well blended
  5. Heat oil in skillet over medium heat
  6. Add some mix to bottom of crepe and roll up
